Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish made of vinegared rice
combined with ingredients like fresh fish, seafood, vegetables, or egg.
It comes in many forms—such as nigiri (hand-pressed rice topped with fish), maki
(rice and fillings rolled in seaweed), and sashimi (sliced raw fish served without rice).
Known for its clean flavors and artistic presentation, sushi highlights balance, freshness, and simplicity.

A smash burger is a style of burger made by pressing—or “smashing”
—a ball of ground beef onto a hot griddle. flavorful crust known as the Maillard reaction.
Smash burgers are typically juicy inside, crispy on the edges, and often served
with simple toppings like American cheese, pickles, onions, and a soft bun.

Lime lemonade is a refreshing drink made by mixing freshly
squeezed lime juice with water and a sweetener, usually sugar
or simple syrup. It has a bright, tangy flavor that’s slightly more zesty
and aromatic than traditional lemon lemonade.
